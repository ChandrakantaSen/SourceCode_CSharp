NOTICE TO MEDICAL PRACTITIONERS
[A]       FOR DELIVERY OF UPDATED CERTIFICATES 2009 - 2010


Updated Certificates upto Registration No.46000 were delivered from Nil Ratan Sircar Medical College & Hospital Auditorium Hall on 25.09.2010, 26.09.2010 and 02.10.2010.

Now, all Updated Registration Certificates upto Regn.No. 61336 (Registered upto 31.12.2005) have been made ready and will be delivered on and from 01.02.2011 from the Office of the Council at the above address (including Registration Number upto 46000 who had not yet collected their Certificates).

Medical Practitioners may collect their Updated Certificates either in person or through Authorised Representative by producing ‘Letter of Authority’ where signature of bearer must be attested by the Medical Practitioner with his/her registration number.

Medical Practitioners, registered upto 31.12.2005 but not yet updated their registration, may update their data on filling up of Application Form and payment of Service Charge as indicated below.
