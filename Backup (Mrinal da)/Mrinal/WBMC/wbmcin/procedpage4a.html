4a.   Procedure   for  obtaining  No  Objection  Certificate
and  grant  of  Provisional  Registration  in  respect of
candidates  passed  MBBS Examination  from Indian Universities other than West Bengal and intend to
undergo internship training in Government Medical
Colleges in the state.
 
 
  Application  addressed  to  the  Registrar,  West  Bengal
Medical  Council   be   made   in  plain  paper   with  the
following    documents   for  obtaining  No  Objection
Certificate from  the WBMC prior to joining internship :

a.       No Objection Certificate from the Head of the
          Institution from where he / she passed his final
          MBBS Examination.

b.       No  Objection  Certificate from the University
          which   conducted   his  /   her   final    MBBS
          Examination.

c.        No  Objection  Certificate  from  the Medical
          Council    where    he /  she   is   provisionally
           registered.

d.        No  Objection Certificate from the Head of the
           Government  Medical  College  where  he / she
           intends   to   undergo   the   Internship  training.
           The  same NOC  should be   forwarded to the
           Registrar, WBMC.

e.        Original   along   with  a  Xerox copy each of
           Provisional  MBBS  Certificate  and   Mark-
           sheet     of      Third    Professional     MBBS
           Examination.
                   ( Original documents will be returned
                          immediately after verification )

After   starting   of   Internship training in any Government
Medical  College,  the candidate has to apply (vide Form
4.1   in    Forms  Folder )   within   7  ( seven )  days  for
obtaining Provisional Registration  Certificate  from  this
Council on payment of prescribed fee, as detailed in Fee
structure.
