Further to the Notifications No. 125-C/WBMC-Elec./83-2009 dated 22.02.2013 & 159/1-C/WBMC-Elec./83-2009 dated 13.03.2013 regarding holding of the General Election of the Members of the West Bengal Medical Council under clauses (g) & (h) of Section 4(1) of the Bengal Medical Act, 1914 (as amended) and in terms of Order of the Hon’ble High Court at Calcutta, it is hereby notified that the Final Electoral Rolls for the Election under clause (g) i.e. Teaching Constituency and clause (h) i.e. Registered Medical Practitioners’ Constituency have been prepared under Rule 5(4) of the Rules made by the Government (published with Government Notification No.H/MA/400/5M-1/88/Part-I dated 20th February, 1991) and the Electors whose names are borne in the aforesaid rolls, are hereby requested to participate in the election from their respective constituencies.  The electors who desire to nominate or to be nominated, may apply to the undersigned for nomination papers in the prescribed forms.
