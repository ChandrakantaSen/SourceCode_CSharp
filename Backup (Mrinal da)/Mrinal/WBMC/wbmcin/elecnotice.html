Further to Notification No. 125-C/WBMC-Elec./83-2009 dated 22.02.2013, in terms of Order of the Hon’ble High Court at Calcutta,  Draft Electoral Rolls under clause (g) i.e. Teaching Constituency & clause (h) i.e. Registered Medical Practitioners’ Constituency have been published and shall be kept open for public inspection under rule 5(3) of the Election Rules in the office of the Returning Officer at 8, Lyons Range (3rd Floor), Kolkata – 700 001 from  18.03.2013 to 10.04.2013

         Any claim for inclusion of a new name or any objection to any entry in the Draft Electoral Rolls, shall be made in the form prescribed in the Election Rules, namely Form II(a) or Form II(b) or Form II(c), as the case may be, (copies of which will be had on application from the office of the Returning Officer).  Such claim or objection shall be filed within the said period namely from 18.03.2013 to 10.04.2013 for clause (g) & clause (h) and shall be examined and decided by the Returning Officer and his decision shall be final under rule 5(3) of the Election Rules.

        The Draft Electoral Rolls shall be corrected in accordance with the decision of the Returning Officer and the rolls thus corrected shall be the Final Electoral Rolls for the election.  In the preparation of the Final Electoral Rolls, registration and restoration after  the afore-mentioned appointed date namely, the 8th March, 2013 or new qualifications or employment obtained after that date will be disregarded and no person, whose name is not included in the Final Electoral Rolls for the election, shall be entitled to participate in the election.  Changes of address after the Final Electoral Rolls have been prepared shall be disregarded [Rule 5(4) of the Election Rules].
